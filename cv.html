Curriculum Vitae

From Word to HTML/CSS

I figured it was time to refresh my resume and started working on it in Microsoft Word, because I wanted it to look like how I imagined instead of just a boring template found online.

I was deep into studying HTML, CSS and JavaScript at the time and found it hard to make the styling look as fancy in Word as I imagined. So the idea came that I should create my CV as a webpage. I loved that anything I came up with was easily achievable and it gave me a great practice in usingFlexbox and the ::before / ::after pseudo-classes.

In the end I was proud of how my resume turned out. You can take a look at it below!
